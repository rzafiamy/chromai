# Privacy Policy for ChromAI

**Last Updated: May 2, 2026**

ChromAI ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our Chrome extension.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **No Personal Data**: ChromAI does not collect or store any personal identification information (PII) like your name, email address, or phone number on our servers.
- **Local Storage**: We may store your configuration settings (e.g., API keys, preferences) locally in your browser's `chrome.storage` to ensure the extension functions correctly. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly sync it with your Google Account via Chrome's sync feature.
- **Page Content**: ChromAI reads the content of the web pages you visit to provide AI-assisted insights and automation. This content is processed locally or sent to the AI model provider (e.g., OpenAI, Google) as configured by you. We do not store this content.

## 2. Use of Information
We use the information collected to:
- Provide the core functionality of the AI assistant.
- Save your user preferences.
- Improve the performance of the extension.

## 3. Third-Party Services
ChromAI interacts with AI model providers. Your use of these services is subject to their respective privacy policies. We do not share your browsing history with these providers beyond the specific content you interact with.

## 4. Permissions
- **activeTab / <all_urls>**: Used to read page content to provide AI assistance.
- **storage**: Used to save your settings and history locally.
- **scripting**: Used to interact with web pages on your behalf.
- **sidePanel**: Used to provide a consistent user interface.

## 5. Security
We take reasonable measures to protect the information stored locally in your browser. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
